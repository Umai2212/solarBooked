Modern voice AI sounds remarkably natural. Your prospects won't know they're talking to AI unless you tell them — and many companies choose to be transparent about it, which actually builds trust.

The system automatically follows up — calling again on Day 2, Day 5, and Day 14 with a different approach each time. Most leads convert within 3 follow-up attempts.
