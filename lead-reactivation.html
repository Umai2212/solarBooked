The Opportunity

What Are Dead Leads, and Why Do They Still Matter?

A dead lead is any contact in your CRM who showed interest — filled a form, clicked an ad, called in — but never converted. Most businesses write them off.

The truth: most of those leads didn't say no. They just fell through the cracks. Nobody followed up consistently. The timing wasn't right. They got busy. Life happened.

Our system calls those contacts, finds out what happened, handles the most common objections, and gets genuinely interested ones rebooked into your calendar — all automatically, all performance-based.

Who It's For

Any business with 3+ months of unworked leads in a CRM, spreadsheet, or database. Works for solar, HVAC, roofing, financial services, and more.

How Pricing Works

10% per recovered booking. Zero upfront cost. You only pay when our system produces an appointment. If nothing books, you pay nothing.
